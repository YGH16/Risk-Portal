SQL Queries Required For The Risk Dashboard:

1. Open Exposure PnL for: Account, Symbol and Group
2. Account Type Statistics for: Equity, Exposure and Balance
3. Symbol Exposure
4. Top 10 PnL - (MAMs, NoMAMs)
5. Bottom 10 PnL - - (MAMs, NoMAMs)
6. Suspicious Account - (Account no, Equity, Open PnL, PnL t-1, PnL)
7. Tick Monitoring - (Come Back To)
8. Top 10 Client Exposure
9 Close Trades PnL - (Between 2 given dates)
10. AIF Sync - (Shows AIF Accounts with Open Exposure)
11. FMT Sync - (Shows FMT Accounts with Open Exposure)
